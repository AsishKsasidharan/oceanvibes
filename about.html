Welcome to Oceanvibes

Explore the spirit of oceans


OCEANVIBES is a non-profitable science community founded by ocean science enthusiasts and students of Cochin University of Science and Technology, Kerala. We created this community as a platform for sharing views, aspects and knowledge in oceanography. The exploration, and understanding of the ocean are often inaccessible to the vast majority of people. By initiating a community of scientists,researchers and students, we want to change that. Ocean belongs to us and let's ensure that everybody explores it.
